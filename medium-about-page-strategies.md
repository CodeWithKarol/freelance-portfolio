Four distinct copywriting strategies designed to convert Medium readers into freelance clients, leveraging psychological principles and the "Modern Angular Surgeon" brand.

**Psychology:** Authority Bias, Contrast Effect, Pratfall Effect

## Strategy 2: The "Risk Reversal"
**Core Concept:** Loss Aversion & Safety
**Psychology:** Loss Aversion, Zero-Price Effect, Commitment & Consistency

**Headline:** "Stop bleeding money on technical debt."

**Body Copy:**
"Every second your app loads slower, you lose users. Every hour your team fights legacy code, you burn cash.
My 'Component Detox' costs $149. A production bug due to spaghetti code costs you thousands. The math is simple."

**The Offer:**
"I offer a **Free 15-min Triage Call** to diagnose your bottleneck. No sales pitch, just a diagnosis."

**Call to Action:**
"Stop the bleeding. View my Fixed-Price Menu."

## Strategy 3: The "Social Proof" Stack
**Core Concept:** Belonging & Credibility
**Psychology:** Social Proof, Bandwagon Effect, Unity Principle

**Headline:** "Joining 50+ Angular Teams in the 'Zoneless' Era."

**Body Copy:**
"I write for **Angular In Depth**. I've helped teams migrate to Signals, Nx, and Zoneless architectures.
We know Angular 14 was rough. But Angular 21+ is a different beast. Most teams are stuck in the past. Let's get you to the future."

*(Includes: "Top Writer" badge, logos of past clients, links to viral articles)*

**Call to Action:**
"Join the modern era. Check my 'Modernization' services."

## Strategy 4: The "Unfinished Business"
**Core Concept:** Closing Open Loops
**Psychology:** Zeigarnik Effect, Present Bias

**Headline:** "Your Angular migration is taking too long."

**Body Copy:**
"You started the migration to Signals 6 months ago. It's still not done. That open loop is draining your team's mental energy and slowing down features.
I close open loops. Give me your worst component, your slowest route, or your stalled migration. I finish it in days, not weeks."

**Call to Action:**
"Close the loop today. Book a 'Small Feature Build'."
